About Us

Our Story

With over 20 years of experience in the real estate industry, we at Virtual Reality have built a team dedicated to helping clients navigate the property market with confidence. Founded in 2018, our firm has grown to become one of the leading real estate agencies in Pune and the surrounding regions.

We pride ourselves on being a one-stop solution for all your real estate needs—whether buying, selling, or renting, our team provides personalized guidance, professional advice, and unmatched market knowledge. Guided by our core values of Home, Happiness, and Honesty, we strive to simplify the property journey for every client.

At Virtual Reality, we believe real estate is more than just transactions — it’s about building lasting relationships and ensuring every client feels confident and supported in their journey toward a new home or investment.
